This Privacy Policy explains how Arrowlane (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) handles information when you use our mobile application. By installing or using Arrowlane, you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Arrowlane is a mobile puzzle game. This policy applies to the Android app identified as com.arrowlane.app and any related services used to deliver levels and ads.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Gameplay & progress data — for example current level, lives/hints usage, and similar preferences stored on your device to keep your progress.
  • Level content — puzzle level data may be downloaded from our cloud (Firebase / Google Cloud) so the App can show and update levels.
  • Device & technical data — information commonly provided by your device and advertising/analytics SDKs, such as device model, OS version, language, IP address, advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID), app version, crash logs, and approximate diagnostics.
  • Advertising data — when ads are shown, ad partners may collect or receive identifiers and interaction data (impressions, clicks) to deliver and measure ads.
  • Optional content you create — if you use the in-app level editor and save or sync custom levels, that content (and related metadata) may be stored on your device and/or uploaded to our servers.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as your name, postal address, or payment card details inside the core puzzle experience.

3. How we use information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve Arrowlane
  • Load and update game levels
  • Remember your progress and settings on your device
  • Show advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Diagnose crashes, abuse, and technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Advertising

Arrowlane may display third-party advertisements (for example through Google AdMob or similar ad networks). These partners may use cookies, mobile advertising IDs, and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads in the App
  • Limit how often you see an ad
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Personalize ads where permitted by law and your device settings

You can typically reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings (for Android: Settings → Google → Ads, or the equivalent on your device). Opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will see no ads; it usually means ads may be less relevant.

For more information about Google’s advertising practices, see Google’s Advertising policy and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party providers, including:

  • Google Firebase (for example Firestore / related Google Cloud services) to store and deliver level data and support app functionality
  • Advertising networks (such as Google AdMob) to display ads
  • Analytics / crash tools (if enabled) to understand stability and usage

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data stored on your device

Some information (such as selected level progress and cached levels) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App generally removes local App data, subject to your device and OS behavior.

7. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, including to provide the App, operate ads, troubleshoot issues, and meet legal requirements. Cached or temporary data may be deleted periodically or when no longer needed.

8. Children’s privacy

Arrowlane is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

If the App is used by a child and ads are shown, we will configure advertising SDKs in a manner consistent with applicable child-directed / family policies where required (for example treating traffic as child-directed where mandated).

9. International transfers

Our service providers (including Google) may process data on servers located outside your country. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied by those providers under their terms and policies.

10. Your choices & rights

  • Reset or delete advertising ID in your device settings
  • Limit ad personalization where your platform allows it
  • Uninstall the App to stop further collection by the App on that device
  • Depending on your location (for example EEA/UK/California), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of personal data. Contact us to make a request.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
