Privacy Policy for Ludo Blitz

Effective Date: March 2026

Ludo Blitz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ludo Blitz mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

Information We Collect

  • User ID, name, and email via Firebase Authentication
  • Device information (OS version, device type)
  • Gameplay data (coins, wins, losses, match history)
  • Usage data (interactions, session duration)

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the game
  • To enable multiplayer matchmaking
  • To improve user experience
  • To prevent cheating and fraud

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics)

Ads & Monetization

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Users may optionally watch rewarded ads to earn in-game coins. Ad providers may collect device identifiers for personalized ads.

Data Security

We use industry-standard methods to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
