Privacy Policy

JobPatra · Last updated: 24 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JobPatra (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) handles information when you use our Android application. JobPatra is an unofficial companion app that shows government job / result related updates from publicly available online sources and provides local utility tools.

By using JobPatra, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information we collect

JobPatra is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

  • No account required. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or payment details for app login.
  • App preferences stored on your device. Settings such as language, notification on/off, alert category filters, and bookmarks are saved locally on your phone.
  • Bookmark cache. If you bookmark a vacancy, a copy of that vacancy content may be stored on your device so you can open it offline.
  • Photos you choose. Tools like Image Resizer, JPG to PDF, Name & Date on Photo, and Signature on Photo only access images you explicitly pick. Processed files may be saved to your device gallery or shared by you. We do not upload these photos to our servers.
  • Network activity. The App fetches public vacancy / post data over the internet from third-party websites/APIs. Standard technical data (such as IP address) may be processed by those providers and by your network carrier as part of normal internet use.

2. Notifications

If you enable vacancy alerts, JobPatra may periodically check for new updates in the background and show local notifications on your device. Notification permission is optional and can be turned off anytime from App Settings or your phone’s system settings.

3. Advertising

JobPatra may show ads through Google AdMob (and related Google advertising services) to keep the App free. Ad partners may collect or use device identifiers, approximate location, IP address, and usage/diagnostic data to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted by law and your device settings.

Learn more about Google’s practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy and how to control ads here: https://adssettings.google.com/.

4. How we use information

  • To load and display vacancy / job update content
  • To save your bookmarks and preferences on your device
  • To send optional new-update notifications
  • To run photo and PDF tools you choose to use
  • To show ads and keep the App free
  • To improve reliability, security, and performance

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by third parties only as needed to operate the App, including:

  • Content source websites / APIs that provide public vacancy information
  • Google AdMob / Google advertising and measurement services
  • Your device manufacturer / OS services (for storage, notifications, and sharing)

6. Data retention

Preferences, bookmarks, and files you save remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall JobPatra. Advertising partners may retain data according to their own policies.

7. Children’s privacy

JobPatra is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Third-party links and unofficial content

The App may open external websites and important links related to vacancies. Those sites are governed by their own privacy policies. JobPatra is unofficial and not affiliated with any government department. Always verify critical information on official government websites before applying or making decisions.

9. Your choices

  • Turn notifications off in Settings
  • Remove bookmarks anytime
  • Deny photo / notification permissions in system settings
  • Clear app storage or uninstall the App to remove local data
  • Manage ad personalization in your Google account / device ad settings

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of JobPatra after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or JobPatra’s privacy practices, please contact us through the app store listing support channel for JobPatra.
