Privacy Policy

O'Sokoban · Last updated: July 22, 2026

Short version: O'Sokoban is an offline puzzle game. It does not collect personal information, does not show ads, and does not use analytics or tracking. Progress and sound settings stay on your device.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application O'Sokoban (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is a single-player Sokoban puzzle game for Android.

2. Information we collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. In particular, it does not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact details
  • Location or precise device identifiers for advertising
  • Account credentials (the App has no accounts)
  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry sent to third parties
  • Advertising identifiers or marketing profiles

3. Data stored on your device

To make the game work, the App stores a small amount of data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Settings — whether music and sound effects are on or off
  • Progress — unlocked levels, completed levels, best move counts, and best times

This data never leaves your device through the App. It is not uploaded to our servers (the App has none) and is not shared with third parties.

4. Internet access

The App does not require internet access to play. It does not include advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social logins, or cloud sync.

5. Device backups

If your device or Google account backup features are enabled, Android may include the App’s local preferences in system backups. That process is controlled by your device and Google account settings, not by the App itself. You can manage or disable backups in your device settings.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is a general-audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children or anyone else. Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not create child-specific profiles.

7. Third-party services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, payment, or social networking services. If you obtained the App from an app store (for example, Google Play), that store may process information under its own privacy policy when you download or update the App.

8. Your choices

  • You can change music and sound settings at any time in the App.
  • Clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App removes local progress and settings.
  • You can control system backups from your device or Google account settings.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App’s features or data practices change. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when that happens. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App’s privacy practices, please contact us through the App’s listing on the store where you downloaded O'Sokoban, or via the developer contact details published with that listing.
