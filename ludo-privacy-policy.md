# Privacy Policy for Ludo Blitz

Effective Date: March 2026

Ludo Blitz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ludo Blitz mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We may collect the following information:
- User ID (via Firebase Authentication)
- Name (if provided)
- Email address (if available via login provider)

---

### 2. Usage Data
We may collect information such as:
- Device type and OS version
- App usage behavior (game sessions, interactions)
- Crash logs and performance data

---

### 3. Game Data
We store:
- Coins balance
- Wins and losses
- Game progress and match history

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Provide and maintain the game
- Enable multiplayer matchmaking
- Improve gameplay experience
- Prevent cheating and abuse
- Analyze usage for improvements

---

## Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

### Firebase (Google)
- Authentication
- Firestore Database
- Analytics

### Google AdMob
- Used to display ads
- May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization

---

## Ads & Monetization

We use Google AdMob to show ads:
- Rewarded ads (optional)
- Interstitial ads (shown after matches)

Users may receive in-game rewards for watching ads.

---

## Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
